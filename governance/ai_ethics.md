# AI Ethics & Governance Framework

MentorIA GovDesk operates in the public sector, meaning it must adhere to the highest standards of ethics, fairness, and data privacy.

## Data Privacy & Confidentiality
- **No PII in LLM Training:** The platform strictly ensures that Personally Identifiable Information (PII) processed during service workflows is never used to train foundational AI models.
- **Data Residency:** All citizen data remains within the jurisdiction of the deploying municipality in compliance with local data protection laws.

## Auditable Decision Making
- Agentic actions are fully deterministic where required by law.
- The RAG system guarantees "Explainable AI"; every procedural answer generated by the system contains an auditable trace back to the specific legal document or ordinance that informed the decision.

## Bias Mitigation
Working alongside IDECON 3x7's legal and constitutional experts, the system undergoes continuous adversarial testing to ensure the natural language interface provides equitable service to all demographic groups.
