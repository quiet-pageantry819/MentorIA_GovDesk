# Research Methodology & Constitutional Focus

IDECON 3x7 (Ideas Constitucionales) leads the research underpinning MentorIA GovDesk.

## The Intersection of Tech and Constitutional Law
Our research focuses on translating abstract constitutional guarantees—such as the right to petition, government transparency, and due process—into actionable algorithmic workflows. 

## Key Applied Research Questions:
1.  **Algorithmic Translation:** How accurately can large language models (LLMs) interpret local administrative ordinances without altering their legal intent?
2.  **Access:** Does a conversational interface demonstrably increase the successful completion rate of municipal procedures among underserved populations?
3.  **Trust:** How does the "transparency of intent" in an AI orchestration layer impact a citizen's trust in local government?

Findings and whitepapers generated from pilot data will be published within this repository directory to support future GovTech grants and institutional partnerships.
